## Notes for Copywriter

1. **Tone**: Direct, confident, zero fluff. Think Bloomberg meets crypto-native. No exclamation marks.
2. **Audience**: Experienced traders who already trade on Hyperliquid or other perp DEXs.
3. **Key differentiators to emphasize**: No KYC, 100% profit split, onchain payouts, permissionless.
4. **Watch for**: "its" vs "it's" (Section 4, Card 2 has a typo: "even though its simulated accounts").
5. **Copyright year**: Currently `2025` — may need updating.
6. **Dynamic values**: Anything in `{curly braces}` is populated from data — don't change the variable, just the surrounding copy.
